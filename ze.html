Zé
Vocals
Zé, the enigmatic frontman of a Death Metal band. His guttural growls echo like ancient incantations, summoning demons from the abyss. Dressed in his shorts, Zé prowls the stage like a vengeful specter, eyes aflame with infernal passion. His lyrics, cryptic and primal, weave tales of cosmic battles and forbidden knowledge. When Zé raises the microphone, it’s as if he channels the very void itself—a conduit for darkness and rebellion. Beware, for his voice is a tempest that shatters eardrums and rattles souls. Zé—the harbinger of sonic apocalypse!
