Cape Torment
In 2011, in the ancient, very noble, always loyal and undefeated city of Porto, CAPE TORMENT was born!
The indomitable will of veterans of the Porto underground scene impelled them to recover the brutality that made them men in the late 80s: Death Metal! Flying the flag of the golden age of Portuguese discoveries, the distinctly old school sound that characterizes them, like Adamastor, mercilessly devastated the public that flocked to the forays they carried out throughout the country and neighboring Spain.
The first volume of their discography, the eponymous EP from 2016, composed of five songs that reveal the full magnitude of the collective's death metal, garnered the most widespread praise from the media and public, with both editions selling out quickly.
In distant Indonesia, the cover was considered the best of March 2016 and they were elected Band of the Month for April 2016, by the editors of Hintf Webzine.
Cape TOURment conquered emblematic venues such as Hard Club, Frenopátiko (Vigo) and festivals such as Mangualde Hard Metal Fest, Viseu Death Fest, Laurus Nobilis Music Fest (V. N. Famalicão) Butchery at Christmas (Covilhã), Blindagem (Vagos), and Carnatal (Cascais), having shared stages with established bands such as Unleashed (Swe), Desecration (UK), Schirenc/Pungent Stench (Aut), Suicidal Angels (Gre) Satan (UK), Lord Vicar (Fin), Picture (Nl) , Sacred Sin, Cannibal Holocaust, Theriomorphic, Analepsy, Grindead, among many others.
With a renewed line-up, the first full-length film and the return to the stage are in the works.
